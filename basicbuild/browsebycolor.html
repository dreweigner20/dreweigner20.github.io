What does photography mean to you?

I grew up with a camera in my hand, so I never really thought of myself as an artist or photographer. My mom would host fashion shows for high-end brands through a nonprofit, and that’s where my love for photography started. I’ve always loved pictures, so I naturally found myself in the background—moving around, capturing moments, and staying busy. That’s how I fell in love with photography.

What's your favorite color?

Personally, I don't have just one favorite color—I love blue, orange, and green. Colors can influence how we feel, spark emotions, and shape how we see the world.
