Green

Blue doesn’t just exist in sadness; it lingers, contemplates, and listens.

It drapes itself softly over quiet moments, letting tears and reflection feel weightless.


Blue enjoys sadness by turning it into depth—a calm in the storm, a space where melancholy isn’t an enemy but a companion.

It finds beauty in the stillness, in the spaces between sighs, and in the soft ripple of emotions that sadness brings.
