Sushi at Ezo

Sushi is an important part of who we are, but it’s not the whole story. At Ezo, we treat sushi as one piece of a larger experience. It’s a way to honor Japanese tradition while celebrating the flavors of Vietnam and Thailand that also inspire our kitchen. Every roll is prepared with care and precision, using fresh ingredients and traditional techniques.
