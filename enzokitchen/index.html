Ezo Kitchen Sushi & Sake Bar

Three Cultures. One Table. Endless Flavor.


Step inside and savor the flavors of Japan, Vietnam, and Thailand at Ezo Kitchen

Welcome to Ezo Kitchen Sushi & Sake Bar, where the artistry of Japanese sushi meets the vibrant flavors of Southeast Asia. Founded by chefs Michael Chen and Daniel Martinez, Ezo brings together generations of culinary heritage and global experience. Our menu blends the precision and artistry of Japanese sushi, the freshness and balance of Vietnamese cuisine, and the bold, aromatic flavors of Thai cooking.

Our Promise

At Ezo, we care about doing things right. That means: Respecting the traditions behind every dish we serve Using quality ingredients that let the food speak for itself Creating a welcoming space for everyone, from families and couples to curious food lovers Representing Asian cultures thoughtfully, with attention and care. For us, Ezo isn’t just a restaurant, it’s a story about connection, culture, and the joy that happens when people come together over food.
