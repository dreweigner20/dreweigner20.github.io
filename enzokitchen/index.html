Ezo Kitchen Sushi & Sake Bar

Three Cultures. One Table. Endless Flavor.

Welcome to Ezo Kitchen Sushi & Sake Bar, where the artistry of Japanese sushi meets the vibrant flavors of Southeast Asia. Founded by chefs Michael Chen and Daniel Martinez, Ezo brings together generations of culinary heritage and global experience. Our menu blends the precision and artistry of Japanese sushi, the freshness and balance of Vietnamese cuisine, and the bold, aromatic flavors of Thai cooking.
